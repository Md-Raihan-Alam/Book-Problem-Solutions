DSL( Digital Subscriber Line ) is dedicated and typically range from 1 Mbps to 100 Mbps for downstream, usually range from 128 Kbps to 10 Mbps or more for upstream
HFC( Hybrid Fiber Coax) is shared, the downstream can be consider from 40 Mbps to 1.2 Gbps and upstream can  be consider from 30 Mbps to 100 Mbps.
FTTG( Fiber To The Home ) is dedicated and typically can handle gigabites per second range
